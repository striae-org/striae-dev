1. **Download the Latest WARP Client**

   * Visit <a target="_blank" href="https://developers.cloudflare.com/cloudflare-one/connections/connect-devices/warp/download-warp/">Cloudflare WARP Downloads</a> and select the installer for your operating system.

2. **Install the WARP Client**

   * Run the installer and complete the installation steps provided by your OS.

3. **Launch the WARP Application**

   * Locate and click the WARP icon in your system tray or menu bar.

4. **Open Preferences**

   * Click the gear icon to open **Preferences** (or **Settings**).

5. **Navigate to the Account Section**

   * In the sidebar menu, select **Account**.

6. **Click "Login with Cloudflare Zero Trust"**

   * Proceed to the authentication screen.

7. **Enter Team Information**

   * When prompted for a **Team Name**, enter:\
     `striae`\
     The full team domain will be:\
     `striae.cloudflareaccess.com`

8. **Authenticate Your Session**

   * Enter your work email address (domain must be registered with Striae).

   * Wait for a one-time login code delivered to your email inbox.

9. **Input the 6-Digit Code**

   * Enter the six-digit code from your email to complete authentication.

10. **Connect to Striae Network**

    * On the main WARP interface, activate the toggle to connect your device to Striae via Zero Trust WARP.

11. **Access Striae**

    * You can now open the <a target="_blank" href="https://www.striae.org/auth">Striae registration and login</a> screens to proceed.

***

**Troubleshooting Tips:**

* Make sure your work email domain is authorized for Striae.

* If you do not receive the login code, check your spam folder.

* If issues persist, contact <a target="_blank" href="https://www.striae.org/support#top">Striae Support</a> or consult the <a target="_blank" href="https://developers.cloudflare.com/cloudflare-one/connections/connect-devices/warp/download-warp/">Cloudflare WARP help docs</a>.
